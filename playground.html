A creative space to explore micro-interactions, redesigns, and experimental UI ideas — from motion experiments to smart settings screens.

Purpose

The Playground is where I test UI/UX ideas in small, focused formats — ideal for quick experiments, creative prompts, or component innovation without the constraints of a full product build.

What’s Inside

Mini UI shots, interactive animations, concept redesigns, and themed components. Some are polish-ready, others are just fun explorations — but all are designed to push visual clarity, usability, and creativity.

JobCard

A refined interface for showcasing employment opportunities, designed to inspire exploration through stunning imagery and clear information.

OntheGo

Designed a comprehensive mobile UI for a travel planning app, featuring essential screens such as budgeting, group trip chat and itinerary management to enhance user coordination and experience.

Ticked

An innovative app designed for students to coordinate projects, enhancing accountability with real-time updates and clear task assignments. This platform simplifies collaboration and fosters responsibility by allowing users to track progress and deadlines effortlessly, ensuring everyone stays aligned on project goals.
