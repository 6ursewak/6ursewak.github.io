WiseSpend

WiseSpend was built to simplify personal and business expense tracking , giving users full control over their financial insights.

Project Scope

Driven by the desire to challenge myself and build a fully functional SaaS product from the ground up, I set out to design and develop a responsive web application for expense tracking. The platform allows users to log income and expenses and visualize spending through charts.

Deliverables

Designed and developed the full-stack SaaS application, implementing features such as income/expense logging, filtering, visual analytics and user account management. Integrated Stripe for subscription billing, marking my first time working with payment gateway integration.

BACKGROUND

Tracking expenses shouldn't feel like a chore.

Many individuals struggle to manage their personal finances effectively due to the lack of intuitive tools. While working on day-to-day budgeting, I noticed that most apps were either overly complex or too limited. This inspired me to create SpendWise — a minimal, user-friendly app designed to help users log expenses, monitor income, and stay financially aware with ease.

PROBLEM

Existing finance apps are often cluttered, overwhelming, or lack essential features.

Most budgeting apps on the market either overcomplicate simple tasks or lack visual clarity. Adding expenses or viewing trends can become frustrating. Users need a clear and intuitive interface that empowers them without creating friction.

SOLUTION

A modern, minimal app that simplifies money tracking.

SpendWise makes expense and income tracking quick and accessible. With a minimal UI, users can log entries in seconds, categorize transactions, and visualize cash flow. It's built for users who want simplicity without sacrificing functionality.
