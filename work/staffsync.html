StaffSync

StaffSync is an easy-to-use app that helps manage employees across different branches. You can add or remove staff, track their attendance and the app will automatically calculate salaries based on logged work hours. All data can be viewed or downloaded into Excel files, making it simple to keep records and manage payroll. The app supports centralized control for multi-branch organizations, offering a clear overview of workforce data. Perfect for businesses that want to save time and stay organized.

StaffSync was was built to simplify employee management across multiple branches , streamlining daily HR tasks.

Project Scope

Develop a mobile application that facilitates managing employees easier for businesses with multiple branches. The ability to handle personnel data, manage attendance, calculate salaries automatically and export data to Excel being important functions. The app should focus on simplicity, efficiency and an intuitive user experience for HR teams.

Deliverables

Designed and developed the full-stack application, incorporating employee CRUD, real-time attendance tracking, automated payroll and Excel export. Collaborated with key stakeholders, refined user experience through testing, resulting in a fully functional app.

BACKGROUND

Manual data management may be burdensome.

While at Samyukta, I identified inefficiencies and errors in managing employee records and payroll manually. To address this, I developed StaffSync using my development skills to streamline employee management, automate attendance and simplify payroll across branches. My goal was to create an easy-to-use solution that saves time and enhance operational efficiency for businesses.

PROBLEM

...but existing businesses rely heavily on manual entry, reluctant to switch to automated systems.

This reliance on outdated methods for managing data often leads to significant inefficiencies. Manual processes are time-consuming and prone to human error. Furthermore, businesses miss opportunities to implement automation or more modern solutions that could improve operations and enhance accuracy. Resulting businesses continue to struggle with disorganization and unnecessary complexity.

SOLUTION

Minimizes errors through automation & features an easy-to-use design.

StaffSync simplifies employee management by automating payroll and storing attendance data in the cloud. While attendance still requires minimal input, cloud storage ensures data is secure and easily accessible, reducing the risk of losing records.
