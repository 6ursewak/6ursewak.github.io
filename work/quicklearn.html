Quick Learn

I worked as a freelancer for AGM Technology to develop Quick Learn, a groundbreaking learning app designed to provide a comprehensive and interactive learning experience for users of all ages. Whether you're a student looking to excel in your coursework or an adult eager to acquire new skills, Quick Learn has you covered. We used Swift for iOS app development and Firebase for backend services. The project involved creating an intuitive and engaging interface, streamlining user flows, and ensuring seamless synchronization with Firebase for real-time updates.
